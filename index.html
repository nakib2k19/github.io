NAKIB & ASSOCIATES

Nakib & Associates was founded in 2001 by Advocate Nakib Saiful Islam, a graduate of Dhaka University and a highly respected lawyer in Bangladesh. Known as one of the leading experts in corporate law, Mr. Islam has extensive experience in areas like banking, telecommunications, project financing, and advising foreign investors. His strong knowledge of constitutional law and role as an arbitrator in both local and international disputes have made him a trusted name in the legal field.


In 2009, the firm grew into one of the top full-service law firms in Bangladesh, focusing on corporate, financial, and commercial matters. Over the years, it has built a strong reputation for handling important legal cases and transactions, both in court and through alternative dispute resolution (ADR). The firm is committed to providing high-quality legal services in a professional and client-friendly way, making a lasting impact in various sectors across the country.
